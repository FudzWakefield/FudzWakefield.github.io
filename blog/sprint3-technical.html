Explain control flow and loops using an example process from everyday life.

Control flow is basically the order you follow when you go about your daily tasks. For example, you have a big game on tonight and you must prepare. You start off with opening your bag and laying out your gears. Your ankle sprain is still giving you grief so you have to strap it up first. The desired outcome is to put on your shoes. Now you cant put on your shoe until you strap your foot and put on your socks. This is the control flow used to get prepared and the loop is that you put on your shoe until the prior steps are done.
