What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the capacity to understand and manage your emotions. The skills that allow you to focus on Emotional Intelligence are Attention training, Self-Knowledge & Self-Mastering and Creating Uselful Mental Habits. By learning and understanding these skills, you will grow in key areas of your life.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ (Intelligence Quotient) is a standardized test which is designed to test for Human Intelligence. EI is based around your emotions where as IQ is an accumulation of test scores that are designed to test your Intelligence.

Why is it important?

Emotional Intelligence is important because if we are aware of it, we can harness these skills to better ourselves.
